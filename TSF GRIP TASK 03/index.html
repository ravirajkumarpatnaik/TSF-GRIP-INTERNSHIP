Poverty is a state or condition in which a person lacks the financial resources and essentials for a certain standard of living. Poverty can have diverse social, economic, and political causes and effects.Millions of children around the world are in need of our help. Many are living in extreme poverty, without access to basic necessities such as food, clean water, healthcare and education. For them, every day is a struggle to survive.But we can make a difference. By donating to organizations that work to improve the lives of children, we can help provide them with the care and resources they need to grow up healthy, safe and educated.Your donation, no matter how big or small, can make a world of difference in the life of a child. It can provide them with access to life-saving vaccines, nutritious food, clean water, and a safe place to sleep. It can give them the chance to go to school and learn skills that will help them break the cycle of poverty.
